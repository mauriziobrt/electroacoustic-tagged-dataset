maurizio_berta

Maurizio Berta is a music composer, researcher and software developer specializing in machine learning, computer assisted composition and sonification of software processes.

He is currently a PhD Student at the Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) in Stockholm as part of the project "The sound of the invisible: Methods for communicating complex and inaccessible information through sound".
He graduated with a Master's degree in Electronic Music from the Conservatory G.Verdi of Turin and holds a Bachelor degree in Electronic Music for the Audiovisual from the "Civica Scuola di Musica Claudio Abbado", Milan.
Collaborated for several years with the ETS association InLuogo and director Valeria Fornoni. He studied with A. Agostini, A. Valle, D. Ghisi, D. Sciajno, S. Bassanese.
Followed masterclasses by Alvin Curran, Bjarni Gunnarsson, Mauro Lanza, Yann Orlarey, Michele Tadini, Kees Taazelar, Walter Grimmer.
